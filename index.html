Impression, soleil levant by Claude Monet (1872)

A groundbreaking painting that marked the birth of Impressionism, capturing the fleeting light and atmosphere of sunrise in the port of Le Havre. Explore Impressionism, and get to know Monet, one of the greats (You may know him from his famous Water Lillies painting)! Try and describe the simple scene, and write down your observations. For a challenge, write out the emotions it invokes in you!

Characteristics: Vivid and Emotional brushstrokes, Focus on Light and Movement, A Revolutionary Artistic Style & Artistic Vocab

Skills Improved: Reading, Writing, Cultural Awareness

La Nuit Étoilée sur le Rhône by Vincent van Gogh (1888)

French Version

A breathtaking prelude to The Starry Night, this post-impressionist painting captures the serene reflection of the stars and city lights on the Rhône River in Arles. Van Gogh masterfully blends darkness and light, evoking both peace and longing. Try describing the calm water and the shimmering sky, and reflect on the emotions it stirs in you. And, enjoy the picturesque Rhône, a vital river in Arles, France.

Characteristics: Gentle, fluid brushstrokes Rich blues and golden tones Contrast between nature and human presence, Simpler to Describe, Strong Colors

Skills Improved: Reading, Writing, Cultural Awareness

Le Rêve by Henri Rousseau (1910)

A surreal and dreamlike painting, Le Rêve (The Dream) is the final masterpiece of French painter Henri Rousseau. The work depicts a fantastical jungle scene, inspired by the artist's imagination and fascination with exotic landscapes. The painting’s strange yet hypnotic atmosphere invites viewers to interpret its symbolism and hidden meaning. A hypnotic introduction into the Naïve Art/Primitivism movement. For an extra challenge, describe this orally to a friend!

Characteristics: Lush and vibrant greenery Surreal, dreamlike atmosphere Naïve art style with simple, bold forms

Skills Improved: Writing, Speaking, Cultural Awareness
